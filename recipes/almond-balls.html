Almond Power Balls

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe for on-the-go snacks when you are in a hurry and dont have time to make a hearty breakfast. This is my go to when I just want a light pick me up.

The base ingredient is almond but you can also add other ingredients to your liking like coconut, chocolate, oats, etc.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup lightly toasted almonds
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond butter
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar

Steps

  1. Lightly toast almonds on medium heat on a pan
  2. While almonds are toasting, add milk, sugar, oats, and butter to blender
  3. Blend until you get a buttery texture
  4. Next, add toasted almonds to blender and repeat previous step (add milk if needed)
  5. Using a cheese cloth wring out most of milk from concoction
  6. After getting rid of liquids start rolling mix into ball size of your liking
  7. Place on baking pan and bake at 350f for 6 min until crispy on the outside
  8. Enjoy!
